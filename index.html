After you finish your PADI Course you will get your electronic certification card directly emailed from PADI Australia.

Here in Nusa Penida we always use boat for diving and never do shore dive as the current can be very tricky. And the price you pay already covered for all the boat diving.

We provide all your diving equipment from your head to toe. You do not need to bring anything but your swimsuit.
